Introduction

About Me

I am a BIM professional with nearly nine years of industry experience, currently operating as a BIM Manager at DSA Architects International in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. My expertise spans multi-disciplinary BIM coordination, clash management, and the strategic implementation of digital information workflows to support efficient, compliant, and high-quality project delivery.

My work has taken me across global markets — from landmark luxury hospitality developments in Saudi Arabia's Diriyah and Qiddiya districts, to mixed-use residential towers across British Columbia, Canada, and educational facilities in Nevada, USA. I specialise in automating repetitive BIM workflows through Python and C# scripting, and in delivering real-time project insights through custom Power BI dashboards.
